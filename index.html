what is this place?

This isn't your program's handout.

The SLPA Playbook is a peer-to-peer resource built by an SLPA who got tired of Googling the same questions with no good answers. Everything here is written from lived experience — not a textbook, not an SLP talking down to you.

This is for SLPAs who are building a real career, not just using this as a stepping stone. Whether you're on your first week or your fifth year, there's something here for you.
