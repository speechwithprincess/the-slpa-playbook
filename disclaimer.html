Disclaimer

The SLPA Playbook is a peer-to-peer resource platform run by a working speech-language pathology assistant (SLPA). All content on this site reflects personal professional experience and peer perspective only.

Not Clinical Advice

Nothing on this site is clinical advice. Content related to therapy techniques, documentation, scope of practice, or any clinical topic is shared as general peer information only. Always follow the guidance of your supervising speech-language pathologist (SLP) for all clinical decisions.

Scope of Practice

Scope of practice for SLPAs varies by state and is regulated by individual state licensing boards. Information on this site about scope of practice is general and may not reflect the current rules in your state. Always verify scope of practice questions directly with your state licensing board and supervising SLP.

Not Legal or Financial Advice

Content about pay, employment, job offers, or workplace rights is informational only and does not constitute legal or financial advice. Consult qualified professionals for advice specific to your situation.

Accuracy

We make every effort to keep information accurate and up to date, particularly for scope of practice content. If you notice an error, please use the Flag Incorrect Information form.
